Examples

Individual food efficiency

Soy is quite good. Eggs are also up to their reputation. Wheat is quite unbalanced.

Food pairs

A sandwich combining bread and cheese (camembert) in equal proportions is almost twice as efficient as any of its components taken alone.

On the other hand, bread and peanut butter may test very good, but exhibit no synergy as far as amino acids are concerned.
