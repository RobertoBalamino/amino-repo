Definitions

  • Efficiency: By efficiency (of some food or composition of foods) we mean the weight of a complete protein that can be obtained with the given essential amino acids, divided by their total weight. This depends on the reference pattern.
  • Reference pattern: pattern of (essential) amino acid quantities assumed to optimally correspond to a complete protein.
  • Limiting amino acid: For a given food, the amino acid for which the ratio to its quantity in the reference pattern is the lowest.
