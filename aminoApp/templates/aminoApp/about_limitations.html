Limitations

We would like you to be aware of several reasons why you should not take the information presented on this site too seriously:
  • Nutrition is a complex subject and there is much more to it than amino acids.
  • Food nutritional data: All nutritional data stem from the National Nutrient Database for Standard Reference of the USDA. However, only mean measured data are used on this site. As a consequence, variations inherent to food products are not taken into account.
  • Amino acid requirements: Amino acid requirements vary with age, activity and other factors. The reference amino acid pattern used in this website may not be valid for you.
